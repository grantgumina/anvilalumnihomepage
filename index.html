About Us

Just a few years ago, some of us were founding members of The Anvil, but today we're all young professionals whose lives were in some way, shape, or form, changed by our experiences at The Anvil. In the beginning of 2018, we decided we wanted to start giving back to the community which meant so much to us, so we formed The Anvil Alumni Network.

Our mission is twofold. First, we empathize with Anvil students who remind us of ourselves just few years ago; young, ambitious, and eager to change the world. Whether these students want to create the world's next unicorn or just want to get an awesome job in a tech hub, we help them by providing resources, advice, and introductions to people we know in our communities so they can achieve their dreams.

Secondly, we want to build a global network of tech professionals who can learn and grow from one another. Right now, our first chapter is in Seattle, WA where many Anvil graduates have congregated. However, we expect to be opening chapters in San Fransisco, New York, and elsewhere soon. If you're interested in helping us, please reach out!

Become a Member

We're looking for fellow Anvil alumni to join us in in building a network of well connected professionals. As a member, you'll have access to an untapped talent pool (students in the Anvil) in exchange for occasionally making yourself available to give advice or mentorship to curious students.
